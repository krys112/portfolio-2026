About Me

I'm a 29 years old who graduated from University of Portsmouth with a 2.1 BSc Computer Science diploma in 2017. I've worked in a bakery/coffee shop (Ole & Steen) until 2021 whilst doing part time software/web development. From 2021 onwards I went into full time self employment, mainly working as a contractor for my friend's companies (MTR Trading, TradeMimic, currently Aphex Automate), mostly as a duo.

As for outside of work, I do my best to stay fit (gym/running). I study a bit of Japanese everyday. I enjoy travelling to towns around for charity shopping, playing video games, watching TV shows.
